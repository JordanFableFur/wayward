Most of Europe shares a single visa zone. Here's the rule:

The 90/180 rule: At any point, you can only have spent 90 days in Schengen within the past 180 days.

It's rolling: The window moves forward every day. Trips older than 180 days stop counting — those days are yours again. No annual reset.

Both entry AND exit days count. A 2-night trip = 3 Schengen days.

Multi-country trips: France → Italy → Spain? Log it as one trip — first border to last. All Schengen countries share the same count.

This app keeps you compliant automatically. Overstaying means fines (€100–€500), possible detention, and a 1–5 year re-entry ban. You're here so that never happens.
